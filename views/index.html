CryptoMine

In this project, we developed a strategic routing protocol that enables a company named CryptoMine to communicate effectively with various locations, including Toronto, Los Angeles, Chicago, and New York. By utilizing different protocols such as OSPF and BGP, we ensured that CryptoMine is connected to each of these distinct sites.
